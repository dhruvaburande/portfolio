About

Writer. Researcher.
Literary Practitioner.

I write with the belief that precision is more valuable than volume. My training in English Literature has sharpened how I read and, by extension, how I write — closely, critically, with sustained attention to what language is doing beneath the surface.

Professionally, I have written content for digital platforms, tutored language and literature across school grade levels, and worked as a behavioural therapist — each role demanding a different calibration of communication.

My creative writing has found its way into three published poetry anthologies. My academic writing has produced a research paper in English Criticism engaging with Baudrillard, phenomenology, and literary theory. I am available for content writing, editorial work, proofreading, and academic writing projects.
